Your Path to Diversification, Growth, and Financial Freedom Begins with Optimus Solutions

Mutual funds, certified managed combined investment schemes, are a versatile and dynamic investment option that pools money from multiple investors to purchase diverse securities. While there's no singular definition, mutual funds commonly refer to regulated collective investment schemes accessible to the public. Identified by principal investments, mutual funds fall into categories such as money market funds, bond or fixed income funds, stock or equity funds, and hybrid funds, each catering to specific financial objectives. At Optimus Solutions, we help you explore the potential of mutual funds, understand their advantages, and choose the right type for your financial goals. Diverse Avenues for Secured Tomorrow

Open-End Funds:

Buy or sell shares daily at net asset value. There's no limit on the number of shares issued.

Close-End Funds:

Issued once via an IPO, traded on stock exchanges. Limited ability to sell shares back to the fund.

Unit Investment Trusts (UIT):

Shares issued once can be sold to other investors. No professional management, fixed portfolio.
