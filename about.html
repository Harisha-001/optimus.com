Brand Identity

OPTIMUS is a Latin word that means ‘one of the best’. We chose this name to reflect the superlative nature of our services and solutions. Our name is paired with the symbol of a five-arch crown. It is a crown of prosperity based on solid foundation. It also reflects the uncluttered and ever flourishing shrub of wealth.

  • The first arch of the crown represents Growth Growth through focused, impartial and professional approach
  • The third arch of the crown represents Fairness Fairness in all connections with employees, customers, vendors and other associates
  • The second arch of the crown represents Integrity Integrity and diligence in all business aspects without any sort of compromise
  • The fourth arch of the crown represents Transparency Transparency in everything we do and why and how we do it
